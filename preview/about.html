As you already know, my name is Laura, a young 24 year old Parisian girl I have just graduated from a double Master's degree in E-Business which completes 5 years of study, 3 of which were spent in internships! During these 3 years spent on a professionalization contract, I was at 148 , a digital and creative communication agency. Initially hired as a community manager, I finally discovered what web project management was and I was lucky enough to be able to continue my internship as a junior project manager

I then learned team management, customer relations, the creative and technical aspects of creating a website, and of course the associated tools. I quickly understood that I wanted to make it my job. Thanks to the combination of my studies and my professional experience, I came out of this experience grown up, determined, curious and full of ambition. I am passionate about web culture, either design trends or technical evolutions. I have managed to create a strong relationship with the teams I work with, which I feel is essential to successfully complete any project. And I've also been able to understand the administrative side of things: contract management, quotes and invoicing no longer hold any secrets for me!

From the kickoff to the testing, through the creative step, the writing of the technical and functional specifications, the back office training and the management of the client and the teams, I learned to handle the whole web production chain and the tools that go with, as : Wordpress, Mailchimp, GitLab, Active Collab, Invision, Zeplin, Figma, Google Suite...
